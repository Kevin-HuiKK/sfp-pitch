Chapter 4 — Pain Points 1–3

No Sales Tracking & Analysis

Pain

No structured tracking or performance analysis. Each rep freelances — no data-driven feedback loop, no accountability, no theoretical framework for growth.

Solution

Sales Intelligence Layer — SFP Drive iOS (AI transcription + coaching, demo-ready) + Growth Tracker & Sales Weekly dashboards (live). Next: live customer signals, manager review tab, cross-rep strategy reuse.

ROI Lever

+25% rep productivity, 26–35% higher win rates. Structured revenue growth instead of random walk.

Salesforce · Gong

Reports Without Insights

Pain

Company generates reports but nobody analyses them. Pure tabular data — management reads spreadsheets but can't answer "so what?" or "what should we do next?"

Solution

AI-Powered Insight Engine — SOM Data Insight + Relation Atlas (both live). Next: AI-generated briefs, anomaly alerts, automated cross-sell recommendations. The system becomes the analyst that never sleeps.

ROI Lever

Replace multiple analyst hires with AI that produces actionable insights 24/7. Every insight traceable to data, not gut feeling.

Money Not on the Cutting Edge

Pain

Funds are tight, yet budget goes to single-task hires. Competitors are cutting headcount and investing in AI. We're still paying for people who can't multitask across domains.

Solution

Hire AI-augmented multi-skilled talent. One data+AI engineer replaces 3–4 narrow roles. Invest in tools that multiply output: AI PO entry, AI invoice OCR ($12–$30 → $1–$5 per invoice), AI coaching.

Artsyl 2025
ROI Lever

Every $1 on AI compounds — $1 → $10 by year 3. Exponential, not linear.

Chapter 4 — Pain Points 4–6

Managers Doing Data Cleaning

Pain

No centralized data hub. Senior managers spend days fighting spreadsheets, manually cleaning and reconciling — doing work that belongs two levels below them.

Solution

Centralized Data Warehouse (BigQuery — already built). Nightly jobs clean, transform, cache. Managers get answers in seconds. Next: AI co-pilot that proactively surfaces what needs attention.

ROI Lever

Reclaim senior management time for actual management. One source of truth eliminates duplicate data work.

Warehouse Capacity Bottleneck

Pain

At or near capacity. More SKUs post-merger = more picking complexity = more labour per shift. Manual picking doesn't scale. We're throwing bodies at a structural problem.

Solution

Short: AI dispatch + wave picking optimization. Mid: deploy automated wave-picking system (vendor evaluation in progress). Long: new warehouse purpose-built for automation — robotic picking, RFID, predictive replenishment.

ROI Lever

AMR payback <24 months, ROI >250%. Handle 3× volume without 3× labour.

Sellers Commerce 2026 · BCG

System Silos — No IT, No AI

Pain

Too many disconnected systems. Every department uses its own tools and spreadsheets. No IT to unify and automate. No AI capabilities anywhere. Cross-dept workflows are manual and slow.

Solution

Unified data + AI platform — all sources → BigQuery → dashboards + AI agents. Next: AI workflow automation (PO → warehouse → dispatch → invoice → accounting), human-in-the-loop exceptions.

ROI Lever

One platform replaces N systems. AI handles routine handoffs. Humans focus on exceptions and strategy.
